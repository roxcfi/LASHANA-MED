Smart babywearing for Neonatal Intensive Care Units

Enhance skin-to-skin contact with a novel monitoring device and provide high-quality data results

Get in Touch

Supervise physiological parameters with invisible technology

Implemented with Smart Textiles the babywearing provides a real-time monitoring and data transfert of newborn's Temperature (T), Heart frequency (HF), Cardiac frequency (CF) and Blood oxygen saturation (SPO2).

This textile-monitoring can be customized and implemented according to different Newborn care department ; preterm NICU, term NICU, Home hospitalization, Kangaroo Care and so on

Empower skin-to-skin contact and newborn's Bonding process

Less cables, more Skin-To-Skin contact. Less stress and reduced medicalized environment to better relationships enhancement.

Improve safety and efficiency

Improve wireless connection to gain data precision and user well-being. The reduction of single use supplies can lead to : a major costs diminution and also participate in the decreasing of skin-infection risks (linked to basic ECG electrodes)

Discover a simple and secure wireless module with NFC technology adaptable to local communication protocols of the hospital.
